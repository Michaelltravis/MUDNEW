# Misthollow World Map

## The Known Realms - Complete Atlas

### World Overview
The world of Misthollow consists of 16 interconnected zones spanning from the bustling city of Midgaard to the chaotic Plane of Eternal Chaos. Adventurers will journey through forests, dungeons, swamps, mines, temples, and otherworldly planes as they grow in power.

---

## Zone Directory

### **Zone 0: Limbo - The Void** (Rooms 1-99)
- **Level Range**: Administrative
- **Type**: Special
- **Rooms**: 2
- **Description**: The ethereal starting point for new characters. A formless gray void where souls first materialize.
- **Connections**: Leads to Midgaard (zone 30)

---

### **Zone 30: The City of Midgaard** (Rooms 3001-3099)
- **Level Range**: 1-10 (Safe Haven)
- **Type**: City
- **Rooms**: 15
- **Description**: The shining jewel of civilization, Midgaard is a bustling medieval city with towering marble temples, busy markets, and guildhalls. The city serves as the central hub for all adventurers.

**Key Locations**:
- **Temple of Midgaard** (3001) - Healing and sanctuary
- **Temple Square** (3002) - Central marketplace
- **East Gate** (3004) - Exit to Haon Dor Forest
- **West Gate** (3006) - Exit to Greystone Castle
- **Main Street** - Shops and taverns
- **The Prancing Pony Inn** (3030-3034) - Rest and rental rooms
- **Grimtooth's Weapons Emporium** (3020) - Weapon shop
- **The Thieves' Guild** (3012) - Hidden underground guild
- **Dark Alley** (3010-3011) - Shady back streets

**NPCs**:
- Temple Healer (Level 25) - Healing services
- City Guards (Level 15) - Armed with longswords and chainmail
- Grimtooth (Level 30) - Dwarven weaponsmith with battleaxe
- Innkeeper (Level 10) - Renting services
- Shady Thieves (Level 8) - Dark alley dwellers
- The Guildmaster (Level 40) - Master thief trainer

**Connections**:
- North: None
- South: The Sewers (zone 35)
- East: Haon Dor Forest (zone 40)
- West: Greystone Castle (zone 50)
- Down: The Sewers (zone 35)
- Special: Portal to Underwater Ruins (zone 130), Portal to Celestial Citadel (zone 150)

## Travel Times & Difficulty

### Beginner Routes (Levels 1-10)
1. Midgaard → Sewers (Easy, 2-3 rooms)
2. Midgaard → Haon Dor Forest (Medium, 1 room)
3. Forest → Goblin Warrens (Medium, 3 rooms)

### Intermediate Routes (Levels 10-20)
1. Midgaard → Castle → Dwarven Mines (5-6 rooms)
2. Forest → Elven Village (4 rooms)
3. Mines → Volcanic Peaks (Long, 8+ rooms)

### Advanced Routes (Levels 20-35)
1. Crypt → Necropolis (Dangerous, undead filled)
2. Portal: Midgaard → Celestial Citadel (Quest required)
3. Portal: Midgaard → Underwater Ruins (Magic required)

### End-game Routes (Levels 30-45)
1. Dragon Domain exploration (Extreme danger)
2. Dragon Domain → Planar Chaos (Portal)
3. Planar Chaos navigation (Reality warping)

## Navigation Tips

1. **Midgaard is your hub** - Most zones connect through or near the capital city
2. **Zone levels matter** - Don't venture into high-level zones unprepared
3. **NPCs stay in zones** - Each creature is confined to its home zone
4. **Portals require quests** - Special zones need quest completion or magic items
5. **Map as you go** - Use the "exits" command to track connections
6. **Return to safety** - Rent at the Inn or recall to temple when needed
