## ‚öîÔ∏è Warrior

*"Masters of combat, warriors excel in melee and defense."*

**Role:** Tank or Melee Damage Dealer  
**Primary Stat:** Strength  
**Hit Dice:** d12 (highest HP)  
**Weapon:** Any melee weapon, shields  

### Playstyle

Warriors are the backbone of any group. They can absorb massive damage as tanks or dish out devastating melee attacks. Warriors use a **Rage** resource that builds during combat.

**Core Mechanics:**
- **Rage** - Builds when dealing/taking damage, spent on powerful abilities
- **Stances** - Switch between Battle, Berserk, Defensive, and Precision
- **Execute** - Massive damage to low-HP enemies

**Starting Skills:** Kick, Bash, Rescue, Disarm, Second Attack, Third Attack, Parry

### Talent Trees

#### üõ°Ô∏è Protection
*Master of defense, protecting allies and absorbing damage.*

Focus on tanking with damage reduction, threat generation, and ally protection. Key talents:
- **Thick Skin** - Reduce physical damage taken
- **Shield Wall** - 50% damage reduction cooldown
- **Guardian's Mark** - Allies take less damage near you
- **Unbreakable** (Capstone) - Survive a killing blow once per hour

#### üî• Fury
*Unleash devastating attacks in a battle frenzy.*

Pure damage dealing with lifesteal and kill momentum. Key talents:
- **Blood Frenzy** - Increased damage output
- **Bloodthirst** - Heal from damage dealt
- **Execute** - Massive damage to low-HP targets
- **Avatar of War** (Capstone) - +50% damage and attack speed

#### ‚öîÔ∏è Arms
*Master of weapons with precise, devastating strikes.*

Precision combat with bleeds and critical strikes. Key talents:
- **Deep Wounds** - Crits cause bleeding
- **Mortal Strike** - Heavy hit reducing healing
- **Sword Specialization** - Chance for extra attacks
- **Bladestorm** (Capstone) - Spin attacking all enemies

## üîÆ Mage

*"Wielders of arcane power, mages command devastating spells."*

**Role:** Ranged Magic Damage  
**Primary Stat:** Intelligence  
**Hit Dice:** d4 (lowest HP)  
**Weapon:** Staves, daggers  

### Playstyle

Mages are glass cannons - incredible damage but fragile. They excel at destroying enemies from range with elemental magic. Mana management is critical.

**Core Mechanics:**
- **Mana** - Resource for casting spells
- **Spell Schools** - Fire, Frost, Arcane each have unique effects
- **Crowd Control** - Sleep, Polymorph, Freeze

**Starting Spells:** Magic Missile, Burning Hands, Chill Touch, Armor, Shield

### Talent Trees

#### üî• Fire
*Master of destructive flames and explosive power.*

Maximum single-target and AoE damage. Key talents:
- **Burning Soul** - Increased fire damage
- **Ignite** - Fire spells cause burning DoT
- **Pyroblast** - Massive fire damage nuke
- **Phoenix Flames** (Capstone) - On death, explode and revive!

#### ‚ùÑÔ∏è Frost
*Control the battlefield with ice and cold.*

Crowd control and kiting with slows/freezes. Key talents:
- **Frostbite** - Chance to freeze enemies
- **Ice Barrier** - Damage absorption shield
- **Shatter** - Bonus damage to frozen targets
- **Deep Freeze** (Capstone) - Stun frozen target for 5 seconds

#### ‚ú® Arcane
*Pure magical power and mana efficiency.*

Raw spellpower and mana management. Key talents:
- **Arcane Focus** - Reduced mana costs
- **Arcane Missiles** - Channeled damage
- **Presence of Mind** - Make next spell instant
- **Arcane Mastery** (Capstone) - +10% all spell damage

## ‚úùÔ∏è Cleric

*"Divine servants who heal allies and smite the undead."*

**Role:** Healer / Support  
**Primary Stat:** Wisdom  
**Hit Dice:** d8  
**Weapon:** Maces, shields

Clerics keep the party alive with powerful healing magic while providing buffs and dealing holy damage. They're essential for group content and devastating against undead.

- **Turn Undead** - Fear/damage undead creatures

**Starting Spells:** Cure Light, Cure Serious, Bless, Armor, Sanctuary

- **Healing Focus** - Increased healing power
- **Inspiration** - Crit heals boost target armor
- **Guardian Spirit** - Prevent ally death
- **Divine Hymn** (Capstone) - Massive group heal

#### üõ°Ô∏è Discipline
*Prevent damage through shields and atonement.*

Proactive healing through damage prevention. Key talents:
- **Improved Shields** - Stronger absorption
- **Atonement** - Your damage heals lowest-HP ally
- **Power Word: Shield** - Instant damage shield
- **Pain Suppression** (Capstone) - 40% damage reduction on target

#### üåë Shadow

## üó°Ô∏è Thief

*"Cunning rogues who strike from the shadows."*

**Role:** Melee DPS / Utility  
**Primary Stat:** Dexterity  
**Hit Dice:** d6  
**Weapon:** Daggers, short swords  

### Playstyle

Thieves deal burst damage from stealth and excel at utility. They use a **Combo Point** system - building points with attacks and spending them on powerful finishers.

**Core Mechanics:**
- **Combo Points** - Build with attacks, spend on finishers (max 5)
- **Stealth** - Hide and Sneak for positioning
- **Finishers** - Eviscerate, Kidney Shot, Slice and Dice

**Starting Skills:** Backstab, Sneak, Hide, Steal, Pick Lock, Detect Traps

### Talent Trees

#### üó°Ô∏è Assassination
*Deadly poisons and lethal strikes from the shadows.*

Maximum burst damage and poison synergy. Key talents:
- **Malice** - Increased crit chance
- **Cold Blood** - Guarantee next crit
- **Mutilate** - Powerful dual-wield strike
- **Vendetta** (Capstone) - Mark target for +20% damage

#### ‚öîÔ∏è Combat
*Skilled fighter excelling in sustained combat.*

Sustained damage in prolonged fights. Key talents:
- **Riposte** - Counterattack after dodge/parry
- **Blade Flurry** - Hit additional nearby enemy
- **Adrenaline Rush** - Double attack speed
- **Killing Spree** (Capstone) - Teleport between enemies attacking

#### üåë Subtlety
*Master of shadows and misdirection.*

Stealth mastery and utility. Key talents:
- **Master of Deception** - Harder to detect
- **Shadowstep** - Teleport behind target
- **Preparation** - Reset all cooldowns
- **Shadow Dance** (Capstone) - Use stealth abilities in combat

## üåü Talent System

### How Talents Work

- Earn 1 talent point per level starting at level 5
- By level 50, you'll have 46 talent points
- Each class has 3 trees with 5 tiers of talents
- Spend points to unlock powerful abilities and bonuses

### Tier Requirements

| Tier | Points Needed in Tree |
|------|----------------------|
| 1 | 0 points |
| 2 | 5 points |
| 3 | 10 points |
| 4 | 15 points |
| 5 (Capstone) | 20 points |

### Tree Identity

Invest 25+ points in a single tree to unlock a powerful **Identity Passive** that defines your specialization!

### Managing Talents

```bash
talents              # View your talent trees
talent learn <id>    # Learn a talent
talent reset         # Reset all talents (cost: gold)
```

## üéØ Choosing Your Class

### For New Players
- **Warrior** - Straightforward melee combat, high survivability
- **Cleric** - Always needed, good at solo and groups
- **Ranger** - Pet helps with solo play, ranged safety

### For Experienced Players
- **Thief** - High skill ceiling, rewarding mastery
- **Necromancer** - Complex pet management
- **Bard** - Group utility, versatile playstyle

### By Preferred Role
- **Tank:** Warrior (Protection), Paladin (Protection)
- **Healer:** Cleric (Holy/Discipline), Paladin (Holy)
- **Melee DPS:** Warrior (Fury/Arms), Thief, Assassin, Paladin (Retribution)
- **Ranged DPS:** Mage, Ranger (Marksmanship)
- **Pet Class:** Ranger (Beast Mastery), Necromancer (Unholy)
- **Support:** Bard, Cleric (Discipline)

## ‚ö° Level 31-60: Endgame Abilities

At higher levels, each class unlocks powerful new abilities that define endgame play. Every class gains 5 new abilities at levels 32, 38, 44, 50, and 56, plus a **CAPSTONE ability at level 60**.

### XP Curve Changes
- **Levels 1-30:** Standard progression (1.4x per level)
- **Levels 31-60:** Slower progression (1.6x per level) for extended endgame content

### Level 60 Capstone Abilities

These ultimate abilities have **10-minute cooldowns** and can turn the tide of any battle:

| Class | Capstone | Effect |
|-------|----------|--------|
| **Warrior** | Titan's Wrath | 10s invulnerability, 2x damage, AoE cleave |
| **Mage** | Meteor Storm | Massive AoE damage, stun, ground burn |
| **Cleric** | Divine Intervention | Full party heal, resurrect dead, immunity |
| **Thief** | Perfect Crime | 30s perma-stealth, guaranteed crits |
| **Ranger** | Alpha Pack | Summon ALL 5 pet types in frenzy |
| **Paladin** | Crusader's Judgment | Holy nuke, party invuln, mass rez |
| **Necromancer** | Apocalypse | Raise every corpse in the zone |
| **Bard** | Magnum Opus | All songs active + massive buffs |
| **Assassin** | Death Mark | Instant kill if target <25% HP |
